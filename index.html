Program

Do you see yourself as a First-Time Homebuyer, but don't know how to navigate the real estate landscape? The journey to home-ownership can be stressful. With our free 30 minute consultation you will talk to a Housing Consultant about your real estate goals. We're here to listen to your needs and work with you every step of the way.

Step 2

Suporter waits 30 days to 3 months for Brooks Brown House complete investment property and return.

Step 3

Brooks Brown House will qualify eligible families for the Tenant to Homebuyer program, after apporval the family will sign to be placed in Supporters Property.

Supporter

Program

By becoming a Supporter you are helping a family build generational wealth.

Benefits:
  • Monthly passive income
  • Wide selection of homes or we build it in your image
  • Continuous tenants who will leave your house in good standing
  • 1 year free maintenance on all properties
  • Assistance in growing your wealth in real estate
  • All houses will be on the same block as investors like yourself which means safe streets and control of traffic in the area
